Administration

From revenue leakage to zero arrears, automatically.

Manual dues collection is GIFF's number one revenue killer. Module 02 automates every step — from invoice generation through to bank reconciliation — with zero manual intervention, eliminating arrears and delivering real-time visibility on every payment.

🧾
Automated Invoice Generation
Invoices auto-dispatched by email and SMS on due dates with configurable schedules per membership tier.
📱
5 Payment Channels

⚡
Real-Time Reconciliation
Every payment is instantly matched to a member record the moment it is received — no manual matching.
🚨
Arrears Escalation Engine

Human Resource
Management

The complete Secretariat people-management system.

This module provides GIFF's Secretariat team with a comprehensive HR system, streamlining administrative tasks and enhancing staff management from onboarding to performance reviews — including Ghana-compliant payroll with SSNIT, PAYE, and NHIL calculations built in.

🪪
Staff Profiles & Onboarding
Digital staff records, employment contracts, document storage, and role-based access from day one.
🏖️
Leave Management
Staff submit leave requests digitally. Managers approve or reject with full visibility of team availability.
💵
Ghana-Compliant Payroll
Automated SSNIT (5.5% employee + 7.5% employer), PAYE progressive bands, NHIL deductions, and payslip generation.

Performance Reviews
Structured appraisal workflows with configurable review periods and documented outcome records.
