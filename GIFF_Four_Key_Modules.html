Module 01

Membership Management

The membership module transforms how GIFF onboards, tracks, and serves its members, ensuring an engaged and compliant membership base — with every record digital, searchable, and real-time.

Digital Onboarding
Fully paperless member registration with document upload, approval workflow, and automatic issuance of digital membership certificates.
Member Self-Service Portal
Members update their own profiles, renew subscriptions, download certificates, and raise support tickets — reducing secretariat workload significantly.
Renewal Automation
Automated reminders at 90, 60, and 30 days before expiry — with one-click renewal and instant payment reconciliation to GRA.
Searchable Member Directory
Instant search across all member records by name, company, district, licence class, or compliance status — replacing physical filing systems.
Compliance Dashboard
Real-time compliance status for every member — licence validity, CPD credits, dues standing — with automated alerts before enforcement action.
Broadcast & Notifications
Push notifications, SMS, and WhatsApp alerts to all members or targeted segments — regulatory updates, events, and member alerts in one click.

Module 02

Dues & Fees Administration

Manual dues collection is GIFF's #1 revenue killer. Module 02 automates every step — from invoice generation to payment reconciliation — with zero manual intervention and full GRA compliance.

Automated Invoice Generation
Annual dues, levies, and ad-hoc fees invoiced automatically on schedule — with branded PDF invoices delivered to members by email, SMS, and WhatsApp.
Multi-Channel Payments
MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo, card, and bank transfer — all routed through a unified payment layer with instant confirmation receipts.
Real-Time Reconciliation
Every payment instantly matched to its invoice and member record — arrears tracked automatically, eliminating the manual ledger reconciliation backlog.
Arrears & Overdue Tracking
Automated escalation workflow — reminder, follow-up, compliance flag, suspension notice — without a single secretariat email being manually written.
Revenue Dashboard
Live collection rates, outstanding balances, and month-over-month revenue trends visible to GIFF leadership without any manual report preparation.
GRA Levy Routing
GIFF levies automatically routed to the GRA Consolidated Fund with full audit trail — reconciliation reports generated on demand for compliance reviews.

Module 03

Accounts & Finance

This module introduces a real-time financial management system, replacing GIFF's reliance on manual bookkeeping and spreadsheet-based accounting with automated, transparent, and audit-ready processes.

Real-Time Financial Reporting
Income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow reports generated automatically — available to leadership at any time without waiting for a manual close.
Bank Reconciliation
Automated matching of bank transactions to system records — flagging discrepancies in real time and generating reconciliation reports for auditors.
Budget vs Actuals
Live tracking of expenditure against approved budget lines — with variance alerts and drill-down to individual transactions for complete accountability.
Audit Trail
Every financial transaction timestamped, attributed, and immutably logged — giving auditors full traceability and eliminating the risk of undetected irregularities.
Levy Allocation Tracking
Full visibility of GIFF's levy income routed through GRA — allocation receipts tracked automatically with no manual reconciliation required by secretariat staff.
Multi-Fund Management
Separate funds — operational, levy, training, and reserve — tracked independently with ring-fenced reporting for governance and transparency.

Module 04

Human Resource Management

This module provides GIFF's Secretariat team with a comprehensive HR system, streamlining administrative tasks and enhancing overall staff management — from onboarding to performance reviews.

Staff Digital Onboarding
Paperless employee onboarding — contracts, policy sign-offs, access provisioning, and equipment allocation managed from a single digital workflow.
Leave Management
Self-service leave requests with automated approval workflow, balance tracking, and calendar visibility — eliminating paper forms and back-and-forth emails.
Attendance Tracking
Digital clock-in and clock-out — with attendance summaries, lateness reports, and integration to payroll for accurate salary calculations each month.
Payroll Processing
Automated monthly payroll calculation — integrating attendance data, deductions, SSNIT contributions, and income tax withholding with one-click payslip distribution.
Performance Appraisals
Structured half-year and annual review cycles — with goal setting, peer feedback, and manager scoring tracked digitally for every Secretariat staff member.
Training & CPD Records
Staff training history, CPD credits, and qualification records stored centrally — supporting compliance with GIFF's professional development standards.
